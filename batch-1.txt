3	Rape	Section 63 – Defines rape, with punishment under Section 64.
4	Theft (of mobile phone or any property)	Section 303 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 years and/or fine.
5	Dowry Death	Section 80 – Punishable with imprisonment of 7 years to life.
6	Attempt to commit Murder	Section 109 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 10 years and fine.
7	Kidnapping from lawful guardianship	Section 137 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 7 years and fine.
8	Defamation	Section 356 – Punishable with simple imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine.
9	Sedition (Acts against sovereignty/integrity of India)	Section 152 – BNS replaced classic sedition; this section penalizes acts endangering sovereignty, unity & integrity of India.
10	Criminal Conspiracy	Section 61 – Punishable based on the gravity of the conspired offence.
11	Dacoity (robbery by 5 or more persons)	Section 310 – Punishable with rigorous imprisonment for life or up to 10 years, and fine.
12	Robbery	Section 309 – Punishable with rigorous imprisonment up to 10 years and fine.
13	Extortion	Section 308 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 years and/or fine.
14	Culpable Homicide not amounting to Murder	Section 99 – Punishable with imprisonment for life or up to 10 years, and fine.
15	Voluntarily Causing Hurt	Section 115 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 1 year and/or fine up to ₹10,000.
16	Voluntarily Causing Grievous Hurt	Section 117 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 7 years and fine.
