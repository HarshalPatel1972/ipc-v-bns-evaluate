#	Offence / Query	BNS Section
1	Sexual Harassment	Section 75 – Covers unwelcome physical, verbal, or non-verbal conduct of a sexual nature; punishable with imprisonment up to 3 years and/or fine.
2	Voyeurism	Section 77 – Punishable with imprisonment of 1 to 3 years (first conviction) and 3 to 7 years (subsequent conviction), with fine.
3	Stalking	Section 78 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 years for first offence and up to 5 years for subsequent offence, with fine.
4	Insult to Modesty of a Woman (word, gesture, or act)	Section 79 – Punishable with simple imprisonment up to 3 years and/or fine.
5	Cruelty by Husband or His Relatives (erstwhile IPC 498A)	Section 85 – Punishment remains the same: imprisonment up to 3 years and fine.
6	Acid Attack (Grievous Hurt by use of acid)	Section 124 – Punishable with rigorous imprisonment of not less than 10 years, extendable to life, and fine paid to victim.
7	Wrongful Restraint	Section 125 – Punishable with simple imprisonment up to 1 month and/or fine up to ₹5,000.
8	Kidnapping for Ransom	Section 140(1) – Punishable with death or life imprisonment, with fine; a specific and stringent provision.
9	Unlawful Assembly (punishment)	Section 191(2) – Every member is punishable with imprisonment up to 6 months and/or fine.
10	Rioting	Section 191(3) – Punishable with imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine.
11	Affray (fighting in a public place)	Section 192 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 1 month and/or fine up to ₹1,000.
12	Bribery at Elections	Section 169 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 1 year and/or fine, or both, depending on the nature of the corrupt practice.
13	Giving or Fabricating False Evidence in judicial proceedings	Section 229 – Punishable with imprisonment of either description up to 7 years and fine.
14	Dishonest Misappropriation of Property (keeping a lost wallet)	Section 314 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine.
15	Cheating by Personation	Section 319(2) – Punishable with imprisonment up to 5 years and/or fine; aggravated over general cheating.
16	Mischief by fire/explosives to destroy a house	Section 326 – Punishable with rigorous imprisonment for life or up to 10 years, and fine.
17	House-breaking by Night	Section 332(b) – Punishable with rigorous imprisonment up to 4 years and fine; night-time commission is an aggravating factor.
18	Counterfeiting of Currency Notes or Bank Notes	Section 178 – Punishable with imprisonment for life or rigorous imprisonment up to 10 years, and fine.
19	Bigamy (marrying again during lifetime of spouse)	Section 82 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 7 years and fine.
20	Criminal Intimidation by Anonymous Communication	Section 351(3) – Punishable with imprisonment up to 2 years in addition to the punishment prescribed for the general offence of criminal intimidation.
