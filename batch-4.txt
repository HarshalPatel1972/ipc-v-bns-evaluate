#	Offence / Query	BNS Section
1	Rash Driving or Riding on a Public Way	Section 281 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 6 months and/or fine up to ₹1,000.
2	Causing Death by Rash or Negligent Act (not amounting to culpable homicide)	Section 106(1) – Punishable with imprisonment up to 5 years and fine.
3	Voluntarily Obstructing a Public Servant in discharge of public functions	Section 221 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 months and/or fine up to ₹2,500.
4	Falsification of Accounts by a clerk or officer	Section 336(3) – Punishable with imprisonment up to 7 years and/or fine.
5	Criminal Trespass (definition and punishment)	Section 329(1) – Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 months and/or fine up to ₹5,000.
6	Sale/Distribution/Public Exhibition of Obscene Books & Materials	Section 294 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine on first conviction; up to 5 years on subsequent conviction.
7	Selling or Distributing Obscene Objects to a Child	Section 295 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 7 years and fine; a stringent child-protective provision.
8	Causing Disappearance of Evidence to Screen an Offender	Section 238 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 7 years and/or fine, depending on the gravity of the screened offence.
9	Disobedience to an Order Duly Promulgated by a Public Servant	Section 223 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 6 months and/or fine up to ₹1,000.
10	Injuring or Defiling a Place of Worship with intent to insult religion	Section 299 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 2 years and/or fine.
11	Uttering Obscene Songs or Words in a Public Place	Section 296 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 months and/or fine.
12	Adulteration of Food or Drink Intended for Sale	Section 274 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 6 months and/or fine up to ₹5,000.
13	Furnishing False Information to a Public Servant	Section 218 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 6 months and/or fine up to ₹5,000, or both.
14	Attempt to Commit Robbery	Section 309(3) – Punishable with rigorous imprisonment up to 7 years and fine.
15	Intentional Omission to Appear Before a Public Servant in obedience to summons	Section 209 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 1 month and/or fine up to ₹5,000.
16	Public Nuisance (definition)	Section 292 – Defines any act that causes common injury, danger, or annoyance to the public or to people in general.
17	Fraudulent Use of a Forged Document or Electronic Record as genuine	Section 340(2) – Punishable with imprisonment up to 7 years and fine; electronic records explicitly included under BNS.
18	Intentional Omission to Produce a Document before a public servant when legally bound	Section 210 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 1 month and/or fine up to ₹5,000.
19	Refusal to Sign a Statement when legally required by a public servant	Section 214 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 3 months and/or fine up to ₹3,000, or both.
20	Intentionally Insulting or Interrupting a Public Servant in judicial proceedings	Section 228 – Punishable with imprisonment up to 1 year and/or fine up to ₹5,000.
